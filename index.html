Frequently Asked Questions

What is English Janala?
English Janala is an interactive vocabulary learning website where users can learn English words by different levels, view meanings, pronunciation, examples, and synonyms.
How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
How do I select a lesson?
You can select a lesson by clicking on any level button displayed on the homepage. Once clicked, all words from that level will be loaded automatically.
What should I do if no words are found?
If no words are available for a selected lesson, a "No Word Found" message will be displayed. Please try selecting another level.
How can I see detailed information about a word?
Click on the details icon on any word card. A modal will open showing pronunciation, example sentence, synonyms, and other relevant information.
How does the search feature work?
Simply type a word in the search box. The website will instantly filter and display matching vocabulary words.
Can I hear the pronunciation of a word?
Yes! Click on the üîä (sound icon) to hear the correct English pronunciation of the word.
Do I need to create an account to use this website?
No, you can use all vocabulary features without creating an account.
Is this website free to use?
Yes, English Janala is completely free for learning vocabulary.
What should I do if I find incorrect information?
If you notice any incorrect word meaning or data issue, please refresh the page or contact support (if available).
